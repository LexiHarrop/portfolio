Coping With Stress

Stress is by no doubt a good thing, but sometimes it feels overwhelming. How do I personally deal with?

Most of my stress is derived from school or task work. When I feel stressed, I go through these three steps:


  1. Stop for a moment, take a breath.

  2. Take a look at what needs to be done, then make a list that has all of those items.

  3. Work through that list one at a time, not thinking about the other things but still not putting them aside.


These three steps really help me with a lot of things and save me from being almost incapacitated by stress.
